Psychoanalysis, now in full color!


Welcome to Psychoanalysis, the game where we test your knowledge of how well you know those around you. It's simple: sort the objects with the person who would associate with that object! The only rule is, obviously, no cheating. You are not permitted receive help from any outside people. You are permitted to use any online resources to help you figure out what the objects are, but nothing beyond that. So, without further ado, let's psychoanalyze your friends!
